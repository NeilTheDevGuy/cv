As you open the door and step through, you are accosted by a giant paperclip.
You immediately recognise it, and shrink away in terror.

"Hello!", it says, in an annoyingly chirpy voice. "My name is Clippy. It looks like you're trying to contact Neil..."

Oh, God. 

"I can help with that. But first, you must answer a riddle.

I am an odd number. Take away a letter, and I become even. What number am I?"

Clippy shrinks away slightly, twitching in an unnerving fashion as it waits for your answer.
